Instructions:

Welcome to Interactive Meta-Balls! There are many controls for interacting with the Meta-Balls and a few of those are somewhat hidden!

- Clicking will place a new ball onto the canvas! (You can control what kind of ball is created down in Create Ball Settings!)

- Clicking on a ball will display that balls information!

- Clicking on a ball and dragging will allow the player to change the balls velocity! (How it is changed is controlable down in mouse settings!)

Tips:

- Make sure that you are clicking and unclicking while the mouse is on the canvas! Some functionality breaks if the mouse exit while its occuring!
